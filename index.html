Spell Me v0.0.1

Spell Me is a Open-Source Chrome extension* that allows you to check spellings and rhyming words as per a keyword.

*Not available in Chrome Web Store

How to setup?

 1. Download Spell Me
2. Open Chrome / Brave
3. Goto Extensions management
4. Enable Developer Option
5. Click on “Pack Extension”
6. Chose the Spell Me Extension Folder you have downloaded.
7. You have done!
